ADFO Summit rebrands to FO Summit

Due to demand, our community and brand will expand further into the Middle East, launching a Dubai and Saudi Arabia Summits. The goal of the expansion is to grow the FO Summit's community outside of the UAE and have more frequent summits, allowing us to build stronger international friendship.
